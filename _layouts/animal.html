Bird Bones - Strong, light and full of air

A bird's skeleton is lightweight, which helps to keep its body weight low for flying. In fact, many birds have feathers that weigh more than their skeleton. The skeleton is largely composed of hollow bones with inner strengthening struts, as shown in this magnified picture on the left of the inner composition of a bird bone.
